About Us

Minali Innovations offers certified organic biological solutions that enhance plant health and crop protection, leading to increased productivity and promoting chemical-free agricultural practices. As an 11-month-old start-up, we have rapidly established a reputation for excellence in the market, consistently delivering outstanding results. At Minali, we craft our products through a unique and proprietary process: BIOPROCESS TECHNOLOGY (BPT). This cutting-edge technology employs selective microorganisms, combined with plant metabolites, to develop safer and more effective products for integrated crop management. BPT contributes to the creation of organic products that enhance ecosystems by improving the carbon cycle. Founded on June 6, 2019, in Bangalore, India, by a team of passionate agribusiness professionals, Minali Innovations was created with a vision to provide comprehensive solutions for organic agriculture. The company is supported by a dedicated R&D team and state-of-the-art infrastructure that enables us to offer non-toxic, biodegradable, and certified organic agricultural inputs to optimize crop nutrition and plant protection. Drawing from extensive knowledge in agricultural sciences and modern fermentation techniques, we named the company "Minali Innovations" to reflect our focus on innovation in sustainable farming. Our portfolio includes high-quality organic fertilizers, biofertilizers, soil conditioners, micronutrients, organic pesticides, biopesticides, organic nematicides, pheromone traps, water-soluble organic nutrients, and plant growth regulators. With agriculture being the primary source of livelihood for about 58% of India’s population, farmers face numerous challenges related to crop productivity, soil health, ecosystem balance, and pest resistance. India’s agricultural exports are often hindered by high chemical residues. Despite farmers’ awareness of the benefits of organic inputs, access to high-quality organic products that guarantee productivity is limited, leading many to resort to harmful chemicals. Minali Innovations addresses these challenges by providing comprehensive organic crop solutions that are cost-effective, easy to adopt, and ensure higher productivity.

Research & Development

Our R&D team is dedicated to continuously innovating and refining sustainable solutions for agricultural challenges.
